Displays weather information as ASCII art from [Wttr.in](http://wttr.in).

## Configuration

```yaml
    prettyweather:
      enabled: true
      city: "tehran"
      position:
        top: 3
        left: 5
        height: 1
        width: 1
      refreshInterval: 300
      unit: "m"
      view: 0
      language: "en"
```
